Return of the Yoda

Your mission is to help Grogu and his friends repopulate.

Decide how many members of the Yoda species will live on Dagobah to start with.

These Yodas will be of reproductive age. Yodas have a high infant mortality rate, so a bigger number is better.

The Yoda species is picky about its food.

They require frogs and mushrooms to survive. In desparate times, they can live off one source.

You must decide how what quantity of food each able-bodied Yoda must procure.

How many frogs should each Yoda catch?

How many mushrooms should each Yoda harvest?
